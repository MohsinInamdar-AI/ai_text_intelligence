Health Information Overview

Health refers to a state of physical, mental, and social well-being.
Maintaining good health involves balanced nutrition, regular physical activity,
adequate sleep, and stress management.

---

Chronic Diseases

Chronic diseases are conditions that tend to be long-lasting and often require
ongoing management. Examples include diabetes, heart disease, and asthma.
These conditions are typically managed through lifestyle adjustments and
medical supervision.

---

Mental Health Awareness

Mental health includes emotional, psychological, and social well-being.
It affects how people think, feel, and act. Maintaining mental health is
important for overall quality of life and productivity.

---

Preventive Healthcare

Preventive healthcare focuses on disease prevention and health maintenance.
This includes routine health checkups, vaccinations, and health screenings.

---

Healthcare Technology

Modern healthcare increasingly relies on technology such as electronic health
records, telemedicine, and AI-based systems to improve patient outcomes and
operational efficiency.
